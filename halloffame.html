    Hall of Fame

    Aviary

    This outpost of Grant Achatz’s acclaimed Chicago experimental cocktail bar, located on the 35th floor of the Mandarin Oriental hotel in Columbus Circle, strikes awe with both view and its high prices. But the liquid concoctions, which reach north of $25, are worthy as a splurge. A Manhattan riff called the “wake and bake,” for example, is infused with coffee and sealed in a bag with everything-bagel scent that perfumes the air.

    Shrek

    A layered ogre who lives in wetland property with custom furniture. Likes to relax and make candles in his spare time.

    The Modern

    There’s no skyline here, but MoMA’s Michelin-starred the Modern — from hospitality titan Danny Meyer — offers a scenic look at the museum’s sculpture garden through its glass wall along with consistently thoughtful food in an airy, sophisticated setting.
